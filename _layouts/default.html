Anthony Wong

I am a third year undergraduate in Applied Math and Computer Science at Brown University. I have a background in competitive math and chess!

Broadly, I am interested in applying probability, statistics and machine learning to understanding thought and behavioral patterns. In particular, I have experience with reinforcement learning, LLMs, and transformers. This website showcases some of the research and projects I've done in these areas.

I have worked with Oanh Nguyen to prove results on stochastic processes and random graphs. I have also worked with Ugur Cetintemel on reinforcement learning for autonomous control in data streaming applications.
