Anthony Wong

I am a third year undergraduate in Computer Science at Princeton University pursuing minors in applied mathematics and philosophy. I am currently a member of the Princeton Vision & Learning Lab led by Jia Deng . I will be a visiting student at Stanford University in the summer of 2025, working with Jiajun Wu.

Broadly, my research interests are in computer vision and enabling models to perceive and reason about the visual world. I currently work on procedural synthetic data for 3D vision tasks. In philosophy, I am interested in philosophy of mind, cognitive science, and consciousness.
